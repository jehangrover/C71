Places Must Visit

>prithvi cafe

i had visited in dec 2018

This cafe is recommended for the Indian cuisine offered. Most visitors point out that you can eat good turkey, nacho chips and Bbq chicken pizza here. Cooks at Prithvi Cafe do their best to provide guests with tasty chocolate waffles, brownies and chocolate cheesecakes. A lot of visitors order delicious Irish coffee, irish beer or red sangria. This place offers you great iced green tea, chocolate frappe or oreo milkshakes.
